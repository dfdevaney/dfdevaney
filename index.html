About Me

Originally from Sioux Falls. I attended South Dakota State University in Brookings, SD. I began my college experience with Engeneering, but eventually gravitated more towards Mathematics. I also enjoyed envolvement with The Pride of Dakotas Marching Band. I recieved a Bachelor of Science in General Studies, Emphasis in Mathematics, Humanities, & Engineering, with a Minor in Mathematics & Religion. I've always enjoyed computers, & even though I had taken a few programming classes at SDSU (C+ & Fortran) I had never really tried programming. After hearing about Eleven Fifty, I felt it was a good opportunity to try it out. Even though the challenge was high, I really enjoyed the things accomplished while learning C#, HTML, & CSS, as well as the various components that go along with those languages.
